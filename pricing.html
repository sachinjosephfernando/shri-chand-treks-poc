Pricing

Transparent pricing builds trust and reduces enquiry drop-off. For the POC, prices are taken from the provided itinerary documents where available.
Fixed departures (per person)
Best for individuals & small groups. Typically includes local transport, meals, guide/support and camping gear (varies by trek).
Private groups
Dates and pace customized. Great for families and friend groups. Pricing depends on group size and hotel/tent preferences.
Corporate / schools
Team building, safety briefings, customized logistics. Requires an enquiry (age range, dates, objectives).
