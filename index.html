How booking works (MVP)

  1. Pick a trek from the catalogue.
  2. Send an enquiry on WhatsApp (or the website form).
  3. Confirm dates, group size and inclusions (transport, porters, etc.).
  4. Pay a deposit to reserve the slot (future: Razorpay link + automated receipts).

What helps double treks/year

  • Clear pricing & inclusions + “What to expect” reduces back-and-forth.
  • Fixed departure calendar (and waitlist) increases conversion.
  • SEO pages per trek capture high-intent searches (“Kuari Pass trek cost”).
  • Automated follow-ups (email/WhatsApp) improves enquiry-to-booking rate.
