Policies (POC)

These are starter policies for a proof-of-concept. For production, we should finalise cancellation/refund rules, payment terms and safety disclaimers in writing.

Cancellation & rescheduling

  • Cancellations must be notified in writing (email/WhatsApp message is acceptable for POC).
  • Refund amount depends on notice period and non-refundable vendor costs (transport/hotels/permits).
  • Rescheduling to a future departure may be possible (subject to availability and season).

Safety & responsibility

  • Trekking involves risks (weather, terrain, altitude). Participants must follow the guide’s instructions.
  • Fitness and medical readiness are the participant’s responsibility. Disclose any conditions in advance.
  • Itineraries may change due to weather/road conditions, forest permissions, or safety considerations.

What’s typically included

Most itineraries include local transport (as specified), guide/support staff, meals during the trek, accommodation (tents/guesthouse/hotel as per trek), permits where applicable, and shared camping gear.

What’s typically excluded

Personal gear (shoes/clothing), insurance, personal expenses (snacks, beverages), emergency costs beyond the organiser’s control, and travel to/from the reporting city unless explicitly included.
