About Shri Chand Rana

Shri Chand is a Dehradun-based trek leader who grew up in the mountains near Osla (Har Ki Dun region) and has spent decades trekking across the Indian Himalayas.

Highlights
  • Local Himalayan knowledge (Uttarakhand & Himachal Pradesh)
  • Small groups (typically 10–12 participants)
  • Custom itineraries for private groups, families and corporates
  • Experience across classic routes like Har Ki Dun, Kuari Pass, Tapovan, etc.
